# Demo Script

Target length: 3 to 5 minutes.

## 1. Opening

World Cup Live Pulse is a fan-first match dashboard for the Consumer and Fan Experiences track. It turns live score changes, key events, odds movement, and AI-style commentary into one readable match pulse.

## 2. Replay mode

Open the app in Replay mode. Explain that Replay mode lets judges evaluate the product even when no live match is available.

Show:

- Score card
- Current minute
- AI commentary
- Market mood panel
- Match pulse timeline

## 3. Key moment

Jump to 80' or 81'. Point out how the score, commentary, market mood, and timeline all update together.

Suggested narration:

The important part is not just showing data. The dashboard explains why the match feels different now, in language a normal fan understands.

## 4. Share card

Use Download SVG on a dramatic moment. Explain that this becomes a social/community asset for Telegram channels, fan sites, or media embeds.

## 5. Live mode

Switch to Live. Explain that this is the TxLINE adapter boundary. Once the token and endpoint documentation are available, live data maps into the same internal match model used by Replay mode.

## 6. Safety boundary

Close with the product boundary:

- No betting
- No trading advice
- No prediction market
- No wallet handling
- No custody or private keys

## 7. Closing

World Cup Live Pulse is designed as a consumer fan experience, not a financial product. It makes real-time match and market movement easier to understand, share, and replay.
