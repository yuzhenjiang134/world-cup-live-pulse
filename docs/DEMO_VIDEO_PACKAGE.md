## Reproducible generation

The scene manifest lives in `scripts/record-demo-video.mjs`. The current screenshots are generated by the final browser E2E and copied to the ignored `demo-assets/screenshots/` directory.

```powershell
$py = 'C:\Users\Administrator\.cache\codex-runtimes\codex-primary-runtime\dependencies\python\python.exe'
& $py scripts\render-demo-video.py --variant A
& $py scripts\render-demo-video.py --variant B
& .\scripts\render-demo-narration.ps1 -Variant A
& .\scripts\render-demo-narration.ps1 -Variant B
```

The renderer uses the local Jianying/FFmpeg H.264 encoder and Windows English speech synthesis. No network service or private project credential is required.
